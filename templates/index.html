This will process payroll for all active employees who have a salary configured for the selected period.

How Payroll Works
  • Only active employees are included
  • Uses each employee's most recent salary configuration
  • Net Salary = Base Salary + Allowances - Deductions
  • Records are saved to payroll history
  • Employees without salary records are skipped
